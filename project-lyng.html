Lyndgaard Media
Media, Public Relations, and Communications Company based in Copenhagen
The Challenge

Lyndgaard Media is a one woman company specialising in journalism, public relations and communications. The owner, Stine Lyndgaard, was starting out on her own after years of a successful career in tv and print journalism.

Stine had an out of date 2 screen business card like website. She needed an entire redesign and rethinking of how to tell the story to keep and add to her client base.

Her website needed to showcase her successful career and bring new clients, as well as keep current clients.

The Solution

As a magazine designer and art director I learned the art of telling a story. I used this experience to help Stine find out how she wanted to tell her story.

I helped guide Stine to what and how to show her newly developing product.

The Process

I started with a wireframe showing how it could look, and what information it could have. This wireframe was used to write all the text for the site.

Upon acceptance of the wireframe I started the design directly with code so we could make sure the site would work well on all screen types.

It needed to work on mobile devices.

I found a stock photo for inspiration. I used this sample to art direct the photographer.

Rational for choosing specific design solutions

I created it as a one page site in order to minimise the need for clicks. We used a screen grab from a television newscast and logos of clients to add immediate credibility.
