About

I have a design degree from School of Visual Arts (SVA) in NYC, and have completed Le Wagon's Full Stack 9 week bootcamp. "Through immersive coding bootcamps, Le Wagon teaches you the skills and entrepreneurial mindset you need to thrive - now and in the future."

These two educations give me the unique characteristic of being both a designer and coder.

I now live and work from Copenhagen. I make websites and SaaS products combining code and design for quick beautiful solutions.

My specialty is desiging with code – directly in the browser. I do this because it saves a lot of time and money. When designing in code, I become aware of dificulties that may arise from a design, and can tackle it at an early enough place, that things can be changed quicker then if recieving a complicated design from a designer, that might not be so easy to code.

Note to Companies Looking for help
I can create quick iterations from wireframes and sketches to beautifully coded websites and interfaces.

Note to developers creating products
With the wireframes created, I can code them into pixel perfect interfaces. I have done full stack work with Rails, Django and React, and understand how to communicate with the backend people.

Special note to CEO’s, COOs…
I specialise in startups needing quick deployment. As a startup, you want the most for your money. Hire me and I will do your frontend design / UI and code at the same time. This saves a lot of time and money.
