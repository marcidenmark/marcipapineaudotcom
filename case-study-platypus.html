Platypus
Sartup company working with Human Resource software
Business Case

Platypus is a Copenhagen based startup working within HR. Its focus is to help companies make good hires, and retain talent.

The Challenge

Platypus was in an extremely early stage of development. They already had a low tech prototype that was used to secure a round of pre-seed funding.

This new iteration of the product needed to move Platypus to the next level of development. It should greatly enhance the experience for early stage users with an easy to use interface.

Project Scope & Objectives

With a 3 month deadline it needed:

» To bring in more funding.

» An interface that worked on mobile. This was both a design issue, and a coding issue.

» A more robust working product with good UX/UI and code.

» An inhanced way to visualize the data– the core aspect of the product.
